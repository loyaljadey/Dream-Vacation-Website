The Two Oceans Marathon

Situated in Cape Town, South Africa, it is one of the scenic runs. It stretches from the coast of Hout-Bay all the way to Fish Hoek around the Cape Peninsula. The ocean location will give runners a feeling that they are not racing and they will calm down because of the ocean's sound and the smell.
