The Tenzing-Hillary Everest Marathon

Located in the Himalayas, this run is only attempted by the best runners. Runners have to wear oxygen masks and thick clothes in order to not die on this 26.3 mile run in the mountains. All runners have is a map of the course and a compass and they have to find their way in the mountains.
